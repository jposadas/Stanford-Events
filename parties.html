SVSA Pandan Waffle Study Break

SVSA Pandan Waffle Study Break

Thursday, March 14, 2013

9 - 10 pm

Arrillaga Family Dining Commons

Come join SVSA for a study break during dead week! We’ll be serving free pandan waffles and green tea ice cream. Never heard of pandan waffles? These waffles are unique because of the use of pandan flavor and coconut milk. You can enjoy these alone, or with ice cream! Doesn’t that sound delicious? So be sure not to miss your chance to enjoy this special treat and chat with SVSA members to hear about our upcoming Culture Night show! We will also be showing Mulan in the background :D
