The Ethics of Wealth: "The Exception and the Rule" (play)

Date: Thursday, April 25 - Saturday, April 27, 2013

Time: 8 PM - 10 PM

Location: The Nitery, Palo Alto, CA

Description: The Exception and the Rule tells the story of a rich merchant who must cross the fictional Yahi Desert to close an oil deal. During and after the trip, the class differences between the money-hungry merchant and his working-class porter are exposed and the prejudices of the existing legal system are shown.
